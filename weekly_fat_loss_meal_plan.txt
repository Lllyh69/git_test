Weekly Fat Loss Meal Plan

Profile: male, 25 years old, 175 cm, 73 kg
Goal: steady fat loss while keeping protein high and meals practical.

General targets:
- Aim for 3 balanced meals per day.
- Include a palm-sized protein source at each meal.
- Keep cooking oil moderate, about 1 teaspoon per cooked dish.
- Prefer water, unsweetened tea, or black coffee.
- If still hungry, add leafy vegetables first.

Monday
Breakfast: Oats cooked with low-fat milk, 1 boiled egg, 1 banana.
Lunch: Grilled chicken breast, brown rice, broccoli, cucumber salad.
Dinner: Steamed fish, sweet potato, stir-fried leafy greens.

Tuesday
Breakfast: Greek yogurt, oats, blueberries, 10 almonds.
Lunch: Lean beef slices, mixed vegetables, half bowl of rice.
Dinner: Tofu and shrimp soup, steamed vegetables, small corn cob.

Wednesday
Breakfast: Whole wheat toast, 2 eggs, tomato, black coffee.
Lunch: Turkey or chicken wrap with lettuce, tomato, cucumber, and yogurt sauce.
Dinner: Skinless chicken thigh, quinoa or rice, spinach and mushrooms.

Thursday
Breakfast: Soy milk, boiled egg, apple, small handful of nuts.
Lunch: Salmon or tuna, rice, asparagus, seaweed salad.
Dinner: Lean pork tenderloin, pumpkin, cabbage, mushrooms.

Friday
Breakfast: Oatmeal with protein powder or Greek yogurt, strawberries.
Lunch: Chicken salad bowl with beans, corn, lettuce, tomato, and avocado.
Dinner: Beef and vegetable stir-fry, small serving of noodles or rice.

Saturday
Breakfast: Scrambled eggs, whole wheat toast, orange.
Lunch: Shrimp, rice, steamed egg, mixed vegetables.
Dinner: Hot pot style meal with lean meat, tofu, mushrooms, leafy greens, and minimal sauce.

Sunday
Breakfast: Greek yogurt bowl with oats, kiwi, and walnuts.
Lunch: Roast chicken, sweet potato, green beans, tomato salad.
Dinner: Clear soup noodles with chicken or fish, extra vegetables, and one egg.

Notes:
- Keep snacks optional. Good choices are fruit, Greek yogurt, boiled eggs, or unsweetened soy milk.
- If training that day, place more carbs at lunch or the meal before training.
- If weight does not move for 2 weeks, slightly reduce rice, noodles, nuts, or cooking oil.
